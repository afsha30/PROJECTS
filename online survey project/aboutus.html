Who Are We!!

We are here to know your problems regarding internet issues.
Online Survey System Project is a
web-based application
developed in Java programming language to
facilitate online survey.
The main aim of the project is to form a platform
to collect the viewpoints of related people about
certain issue using the internet.
Along with launching survey,
it is capable of giving e-mail notifications.
It can be implemented in any organizations
or college for carrying out survey of any kind.
